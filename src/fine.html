Library Fines

The Departmental Library assesses a fine to the record of any student who fails to return library materials on or before their due date. Accumulated fines shall result in the suspension of borrowing privileges. The fine is calculated depending on the number of days taken to return the books after the due dates. Fine rate: Rs 10/- per book per day.
